Machining Aesthetic Exhibition Display
Photography: Richard Maddock 2013.

The Machining Aesthetic Exhibition was held at Craft for the duration of the Craft Cubed Festival in August 2013.

Project Team

Marc Micuta

Technical Skills

Rhino 3D

Grasshopper

Prototyping - CNC 3 Axis Mill

Fabrication - CNC 3 Axis Mill

The design was developed with Rhino 3D and the Grasshopper plug-in to be a responsive and infinitely customisable system. Prototypes were fabricated at 1:5 to test out the joint system, structural performance and assembly tolerances.

The final product was fabricated with a CNC mill over the course of 3 days and was assembled in 4 hours. The design is held together with friction / lap joints that were milled out of the planar elements, which allows the structure to be demountable.
